# Scholr

**Scholr** is an AI-powered academic operating system designed to help university students stay organized, study smarter, and achieve their academic goals.

Rather than juggling multiple apps for note-taking, planning, grade tracking, and studying, Scholr brings everything together in one intuitive platform.

## ✨ Features

* 🤖 **24/7 AI Tutor** – Upload lecture notes and receive personalized explanations.
* 📊 **Grade Analytics** – Monitor your academic progress and predict your performance.
* 📚 **Course Dashboard** – Manage courses, assignments, and deadlines in one place.
* 📄 **Lecture Uploads** – Store and organize lecture slides and study materials.
* ⏱️ **Pomodoro Study Timer** – Stay focused with built-in study sessions.
* 📝 **Practice Tests** – Reinforce learning with quizzes generated from your course materials.

## 🎯 Mission

Our mission is to make academic success simpler, more accessible, and more personalized by giving students one platform for everything they need to succeed.
